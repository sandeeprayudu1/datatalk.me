About Us

Datatalk is about gathering all the data scientists to share blogs and projects on datascience.



Datatalk is done for personal blog applicaion and later it turned to gather all data scientists.The main goal of data talk is share the knowledge of data science who are eager to learn.we will try to write blogs on every topic.



Datatalk originated from the idea that there exists a class of bloggers who respond better to online content and prefer to write and as well as learn datascience skills at their own pace from the comforts of their drawing rooms.What we built is just a begining and our doors are always open to join with us to developed.
